TBLI EXPERTS

ARNOLD TSUNGA

Chairperson and Principal Managing Partner heads TBLI international litigation, consultancy and advisory portfolio. He is one of the very unique lawyers whose experience spans private and public practice in law, politics and public administration, international NGO leadership and management, and sports administration and management. He was registered to practice law in 1990 and has remained in good standing since. A past Resident Senior Director at the National Democratic Institute (NDI) in Zimbabwe and past Africa Director of the International Commission of Jurists (ICJ), he has competence and expertise in leadership, human rights and the rule of law; impact/strategic/public interest litigation; elections and the rule of law, public administration and policy; leadership and management; strategic planning, organisational development and institution building; project management (including planning, implementing, monitoring and evaluation); mentoring and performance evaluation, movement and network building, donor relations and resource mobilisation. This experience and competencies influences the parameters of work and practice areas for TBLI in Zimbabwe and across Africa. Arnold has received international recognition for his international development work including the Martin Ennals Award and the Hubert Humphrey Fellowship.

JEREMIAH MUTONGI BAMU

The Managing Partner and Head of the Litigation Department at TBLI is an award-winning human rights lawyer with over 15 years of experience as a litigator, community trainer, and human rights defender. Specializing in constitutional, labor, and human rights law, Bamu has established himself as a champion for the protection and advancement of civil liberties in Zimbabwe. A graduate of the University of Zimbabwe, Bamu distinguished himself early in his career, being recognized as the Young Human Rights Lawyer of the Year by the Law Society of Zimbabwe in 2011. His commitment to human rights was further affirmed when he was selected as a Mandela Washington Fellow, part of the Young African Leaders Initiative founded by former U.S. President Barack Obama. In his professional work, Bamu has provided rights-based litigation and advisory services, spearheaded creative community awareness initiatives, and conducted rigorous rights-based research and advocacy. He has worked as an associate at leading law firms and served as a Senior Projects Lawyer at the Zimbabwe Lawyers for Human Rights (ZLHR), where he was part of the organization's Human Rights Defenders Unit. Bamu's expertise, accolades, and track record of impact, position him as a formidable voice for justice and a trusted ally of human rights defenders in Zimbabwe and the broader region.

DR. PROSPER SIMBARASHE MAGUCHU

Head of Training, Capacity Building and Professional Development at TBLI is a seasoned lawyer, researcher and lecturer with extensive experience across Africa, Europe, and Asia. He seamlessly blends his academic expertise with practical application, working in both the civil society and higher education sectors. He lectures at universities, coordinates international law clinics and mooting programs, and equips students with both in-depth knowledge and practical skills in international litigation. He designs and delivers tailor-made training programs for government departments and private sector entities, focusing on financial crimes such as corruption, money laundering, international asset recovery strategies, and navigating the complexities of sanctions. His experience extends beyond academia, as he actively consults with prominent regional and international organizations in the area of law and development.

MAKOMBORERO SAMANTHA CHITSA

TBLI professional associate, holds a Bachelor of Laws (Hons) Degree from the University of Zimbabwe. In addition to being a registered legal practitioner with the Superior Courts of Zimbabwe, she is also a registered Notary Public and Conveyancer. Makomborero is a passionate legal professional dedicated to pursuing a career specializing in Criminal Law, focused on criminal justice and advocacy in all courts in Zimbabwe. She also has immense interest in civil law litigation on top of the criminal law portfolio and specialization in criminal trial advocacy. Makomborero is also well-versed in the intricacies of contract law and stands to serve as a trusted advisor to businesses in drafting, negotiating, and reviewing various commercial agreements. In so doing, she is dedicated to ensuring that the firm's clients' interests are always safeguarded. She has a niche for excellence and quality legal work at all times.

TAFARA GORO

the TBLI expert and lead on Labour Law holds an LLB (Hons) Degree from the University of Zimbabwe where he graduated top of his class and received the Law Society of Zimbabwe award for best law student of 2015. He was admitted to practice as Legal Practitioner, Conveyancer and Notary Public in October 2015. He has been in private practice since then. In 2018 Tafara joined Mbidzo Muchadehama and Makoni as an associate in the Labour, Constitutional & Rights Services Department. He joined TBLI in 2024. He has vast interest and experience in research, general litigation and an asset for corporate advisory services.

MITCHELL MAMIMINE

The TBLI expert and lead on Family, Estate Planning and Succession holds an LLB (Hons) and LLM in Constitutional and Human Rights Law, both attained with the Midlands State University in 2017 and 2022 respectively. She has previously worked with Judicial Service Commission (JSC) as a Research Assistant in the Supreme and Constitutional Court where she researched for Justice Mavangira, Makoni, Makarau and Deputy Chief Justice Gwaurza. She also worked as a legal associate at Messrs Mbidzo Muchadehama and Makoni Legal Practitioners from 2021 involved in litigation in various courts and tribunals of Zimbabwe.She has a passion for family law and commercial law.
