Who are we

TSUNGA BAMU LAW INTERNATIONAL (TBLI) is a law practice established in Zimbabwe on 12 July 2023 and recognized as such by the Law Society of Zimbabwe as the regulating authority under the Legal Practitioners Act (CAP27:07).
